Nightfall With Edgar Allen Poe
By Eric Coble

"Nightfall with Edgar Allen Poe" is produced with special arrangement with Playscripts, Inc.

June 17th-July 30th

You, our audiences, have been asking for this show for over a decade.

Synopsis:

For October, we bring you the four tales that Poe himself thought were his most spooky: The Raven, The Fall of the House of Usher, The Pit and the Pendulum, and The Tell-Tale Heart. One actor portrays Poe as we live through his scariest tales. Find us at an outdoor venue near you for these spooky tales among the autumn leaves.
