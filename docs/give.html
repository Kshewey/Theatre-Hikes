Interested in helping Theatre-Hikes?

What makes us unique... besides walking while performing, OUTDOORS??

We pay ALL artists who create this entertainment! This includes actors, many companies our size cannot pay actors. We are lucky to work with outdoor venues and don't spend as much on performance space rental or scenery. So we put that savings towards giving artists a chance at making money with their art.
